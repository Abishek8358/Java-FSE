Arts & Crafts Festival

Celebrating local creativity, pottery, and canvas painting

The annual Arts & Crafts Festival invites you to discover over 50 galleries showcasing handmade sculptures, custom textiles, and original prints. Meet local artists, participate in live woodturning sessions, and explore interactive canvas painting workshops open to all skill levels. Kids' craft zones and local acoustic performances run throughout the day.

Typography Rules In Use:

  • Font Family: Imported Google Font 'Space Grotesk' via @import inside the external CSS.
  • Text Alignment: Banner titles are centered (text-align: center), and the description is justified (text-align: justify) for clean paragraph margins.
  • Casing: Title is uppercase (text-transform: uppercase), and accent labels are uppercase.
  • Spacing: Title letter spacing is set to 3px, and the description line height is styled with 1.8 for enhanced reading comfort.
  • Font Style & Weight: Subtitle is styled with font-style: italic and a thin font weight of 300.
